## Overview

A custom WordPress site for Filipino solo artist Gelo Cruz. The layout puts media front and centre — releases, performances, and press coverage — with Swiper-powered sliders doing the heavy lifting for the carousels. Tailwind keeps the brand tight and the design responsive across the sizes an artist site actually gets shared at, from phone to projector.
