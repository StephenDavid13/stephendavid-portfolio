## Overview

A custom WordPress theme for Whitecomb Range, an outdoor experience ranch. The core build was a booking system covering three distinct product lines — accommodation, training courses, and events — each with its own availability logic and enquiry flow. Styled mobile-first with Tailwind and scaffolded with WooCommerce ready to switch on when the ranch moves to direct online sales.
